Shamsiye Schools

SHAMSIYE BOYS SCHOOL is an independent, private-owned, Islamic boarding school located in Mbweni-Mpiji Kinondoni Dar es Salaam, TANZANIA.

School offers rigorous academic programs coupled with a wide range of extra curriculum activities serving students from Form 1 to Form 6 with Four combinations; PCM, PCB, EGM & CBG.

About US

Historical Background

Shamsiye Schools was established in 2013, with form 1 and form 2 classes. later on in 2017 the first form five students were enrolled. This was under the first Shamsiye headmaster Alli Karakaya.
Shamsiye is located at Mbweni-Mpiji Dar es Salaam

Education

Shamsiye offers Primary, Secondary and Advanced level education.
Shamsiye admits islamic students and uses Necta curriculum

Staff

Shamsiye has an approximately number of 54 staff members inluding teaching and non-teaching staff. at Shamsiye students learn religious and non-religious subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and many more.
